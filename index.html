About

Some people build for today. Fran builds for what comes after.

Trained as an architect, he learned early that the most powerful designs aren't made of concrete — they're made of systems, ideas, and the cultures that carry them forward. A building has a lifespan. The right idea doesn't.

That conviction drove a decade leading ICON University, where he redesigned not the campus but the very model of what a university could be. It took him to Singularity University in Silicon Valley, where exponential thinking stopped being a concept and became a method. And it brought him to found ZXY Ventures — not as a company, but as a living system built to outlast its own creator.

Today, ZXY operates as an agentic venture studio: a small team of AI agents building real products for real problems in Latin America. Each one designed to run without him. Each one a small proof that the right architecture doesn't need you to keep showing up.
