About

Some people build for today. Fran builds for what comes after.

Trained as an architect, he learned early that the most powerful designs aren't made of concrete — they're made of ideas and the cultures that carry them forward. A building has a lifespan. The right idea doesn't.

That drove a decade leading ICON University — redesigning not the campus but the model itself. Then Singularity University, where exponential thinking became a method. Then ZXY Ventures: not a company, but a living system built to outlast its own creator.

Today, ZXY is an agentic venture studio: AI agents building real products for real problems in Latin America. Each one designed to run without him. Proof that the right architecture doesn't need you to keep showing up.
